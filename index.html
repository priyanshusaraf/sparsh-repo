Who We Are

Built on Passion,
Driven by Innovation

Sparsh Gem Plast Pvt Ltd is a leading name in the manufacturing and wholesale distribution of high-quality plastic products. With over 25 years of expertise, we have built a reputation for excellence, durability, and design innovation.

Operating under the flagship brand SPARSH — "Plastic Jo Dil ke Kareeb" — we bring products that seamlessly blend functionality and style for homes, businesses, and industries alike.

Headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal, our two manufacturing units and an expansive product portfolio of 450+ items make us one of the most comprehensive plastic product companies in India.
